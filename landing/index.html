· Capabilities

What makes it different.

↗

Conflict surfacing

Two sources disagree → both kept with a Disputed badge. The argument surfaces; nothing is silently averaged.

↗

Temporal queries

"Refund cap today?" $1,000. "Refund cap in April?" $2,000. Old policy marked [SUPERSEDED] — both answers correct.

↗

Slack-native ingest

/ask, /ingest-channel, /ingest-thread. HMAC signature verification on every request. Grounded answers in-thread.

↗

Multimodal VLM

Qwen2-VL 7B reads architecture diagrams, whiteboard photos, org charts. Fuses with text in the same graph.

↗

Gap detection

Pure graph topology scan: missing_owner · undescribed_entity · orphan_gotcha · open_dispute. Deterministic — no LLM call.
